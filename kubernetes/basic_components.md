## Basic Components Of K8s

**The following are basic components of k8**
- Master node 
- Worker node
- Pods
- Service
- Volume
- Labels and selectors
- Namespaces

**Other Important components of k8**
- Ingress
- StatefulSets
- Persistent Volume, Persistent Volume Claims
- ConfigMaps
- Secrets
- ReplicaSets
- Deployments
